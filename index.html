Tee Laboratory

Lab Focus

The Tee Laboratory seeks to understand how cells sense, integrate, and adapt to metabolic inputs in changing physiological and pathological environments.

By combining quantitative imaging, molecular profiling, and systems-level analysis, we aim to develop non-invasive approaches that link metabolic state to cellular function, tissue remodeling, and disease progression.
